The way to grow is by not wasting time!
R' Yosef Shmerelovitz

This website is designed to teach you to spend your time learning תורה instead of wasting it sitting on the computer, therefore I request of you to click on this link to enjoy a host of שיעורים for any and every Jew. These שיעורים span from the most beginner friendly to the most advanced.How about if you learn some Torah tonight? (this link will open in another window)It will really enhance your life!! רש"י (ה') "ואם תאמר מפני מה השרה הקדוש ברוך הוא שכינתו על גוי רשע, כדי שלא יהא פתחון פה לאמות לומר אלו היו לנו נביאים חזרנו למוטב, העמיד להם נביאים והם פרצו גדר העולם, שבתחלה היו גדורים בערי ות וזה נתן להם עצה להפקיר עצמן לזנות" We know that בלעם was a רשע who was involved in the ג' חמורות; what type of answer is that for a גוי who’s honestly looking to do the right thing? בלעם won’t convince him to do the right thing; quite the contrary. It would seem from here that ה' i trying to tell them that נבואה isn’t a silver bullet. בלעם was a very respected person. He was פרעה’s advisor and a confidant of many kings. רש"י (שם) says on פתורה that kings would ask for his advice. It would seem that his serious flaws didn’t bother them. They respected him in spite of his very low character. He was the man that society looked up to. הקב"ה was showing them that נבואה is only a tool. If you type the wrong address into the GPS you won’t ever reach your destination True ה' gave them נבואה; but it was up to them to choose the destination. If what they really respected, was something other than what’s right that’s what they’ll get. בדרך שאדם רוצה לילך בה מוליכין אותו.The lesson we see from here is that we need to always know our priorities and want what’s right. Do not blame externalities. Without wanting to do what’s right all the outside factors won’t do it. Listen and Enjoy!!
